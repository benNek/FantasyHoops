Game score (GS) = (Points)+0.4*(Field Goals Made)+0.7*(Offensive Rebounds)+0.3*(Defensive rebounds)+(Steals)+0.7*(Assists)+0.7*(Blocked Shots)- 
0.7*(Field Goal Attempts)-0.4*(Free Throws Missed) – 0.4*(Personal Fouls)-(Turnovers)

Fantasy points per game (FPPG) = 1*(Points)+1.2*(Rebounds)+1.5*(Assists)+3*(Steals)+3*(Blocks)-1*(Turnovers)

FPPG prades skaiciuoti nuo sezono pradzios sudedant kiekvienu rungtyniu Fantasy Points ir gaunant ju vidurki,
praejus vienoms varzyboms, vidurkis perskaiciuojamas

Kad gauti zaidejo kaina, reikia apskaiciuoti zaidejo paskutiniu 5 rungtyniu game score vidurki (GSvid), prie jo prideti
fantasy points per game ir suma padauginti is 1300

Zaidejo kaina = (GSvid + FPPG) * 1300
